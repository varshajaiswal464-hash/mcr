ABOUT US

Marwari College, Ranchi is one of the premier institutions of higher education in Jharkhand. Established in 1963 by the Marwari Shiksha Trust, the college was founded with the vision of providing quality education to students from all sections of society, especially the tribal, rural, and economically weaker communities. The institution came into existence through the dedicated efforts of Ganga Prasad Budhia.

Over the years, the college has grown from a small institution with a few hundred students into a renowned autonomous college offering undergraduate, postgraduate, vocational, and professional courses in Arts, Commerce, Science, and Management. It became a constituent unit of Ranchi University in 1980 and has received recognition from the UGC along with NAAC accreditation for academic excellence.

Vision:

To create a quality teaching-learning environment that nurtures knowledge, skills, leadership, and social responsibility, enabling students to contribute positively to society.

Mission:

To improve graduate outcomes.
To provide quality and inclusive education.
To develop professional, technical, and soft skills among students.
To promote ethical values, leadership, and nation-building.
Today, Marwari College, Ranchi is recognized as a leading educational institution dedicated to academic excellence, skill development, innovation, and the holistic growth of students.
